Core platform capability

MetaFlow acts as the agentic decision layer between planning-system outputs and governed inventory parameter actions.

🔌

Connect Data

Connect inventory parameter data from ERP, planning platforms, optimization engines, and analytical data stores. Bring together the current planning context required for SKU-location decisions.

🧭

Review Changes

Ingest recommended safety stock and lot-size changes, match them to current parameters, identify risk, and guide planners to the decisions that need attention.

⚖️

Capture Decisions

Approve, reject, or escalate recommendations with reason capture, rejection categorization, decision history, and recommendation-level audit visibility.

What makes the platform useful

MetaFlow turns inventory parameter recommendations into explainable, traceable, and auditable decisions.

💡

Explainability

Shows why a recommendation may matter, what data was available, and what trade-offs the planner should consider before approving or rejecting the change.

🧮

What-if Analysis

Estimates inventory value change, working capital impact, lot-size change, and service risk direction using transparent decision-support calculations.

🧾

Decision History

Captures who approved, rejected, or escalated a recommendation, along with reason text, rejection category, assignee, and decision timestamp.

🛡️

Audit Visibility

Recommendation-level audit events make the workflow reviewable, explainable, and ready for operational governance.

MetaFlow Positioning: MetaFlow does not replace enterprise planning systems. It operates as an agentic SaaS governance layer above planning and optimization outputs, helping teams connect data, review changes, capture decisions, and maintain an audit-ready record of inventory parameter actions.
